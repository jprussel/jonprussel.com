Skream at Moda Leicester

Poster | Ticket | CD Cover | Sticker | VIP Pass

LiveTour UK, a music production company based in London, worked with electronic artist Skream to create a live recording of his show at Moda Leicester. Skream is known for his improvisational music stylings, but his commercially produced tracks lack that. Varied line style, elements that come off the page, and a space theme all contribute to the magic of Skream's live performances.
