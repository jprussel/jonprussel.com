FlightStats Mobile App

Mobile App for iOS (iPhone and Apple Watch) and Android

FlightStats, a provider of global flight data, needed an updated mobile app to both add new features and implement the new branding of their apps. The FlightStats app lets a user search for and track the status of commercial flights, look up airport conditions, and save their flight searches for future reference. With the release of the Apple Watch, an extension was added to the iOS app to include flight tracking for Apple Watch based on saved flights in the FlightStats app. Much of this content was then used to create the FlightStats mobile website, which offered similiar functionality, for those who would not (or could not) download the app.
